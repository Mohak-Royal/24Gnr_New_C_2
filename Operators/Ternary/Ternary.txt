Ternary Operators:

This operator is a conditional operator which is used to perform conditional operations.


for Example : eligible voting , maximum number



Syntax :   

(condition)?  expression_if_true : expression_if_false

for Example : maximum of two numbers

a = 10 , b = 5

(a > b)?   printf("a is maximum") : printf("b is maximum");

output : a is maximum
